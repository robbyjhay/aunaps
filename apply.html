Apply for Admission

Begin your child's educational journey with ANCHOR UNIVERSITY NURSERY AND PRIMARY SCHOOL.

Admission Process

  1. Online Application: Fill out the admission form below with all required details.
  2. Document Submission: Upload or submit scanned copies of necessary documents (birth certificate, previous academic records, passport photographs).
  3. Entrance Assessment: Applicants will be invited for an age-appropriate assessment (for Nursery and Primary).
  4. Interview: A brief interview may be conducted with the child and parents/guardians.
  5. Admission Offer: Successful candidates will receive an admission offer letter.
  6. Enrollment & Payment: Complete the enrollment process by paying the required fees by the stipulated deadline.

Required Documents

  • Child's Original Birth Certificate (for verification)
  • Photocopy of Child's Birth Certificate
  • Two recent Passport Photographs of the Child
  • One recent Passport Photograph of Each Parent/Guardian
  • Immunization Record/Health Certificate
  • Previous Academic Records/Report Cards (for Primary applicants)
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
